Hi I am Alptekin,

I was born on a cold March morning in Ünye district of Ordu province. After staying in Fatsa district until 1 year old, we came to Adapazarı district of Sakarya. Later, we settled in Karaçam village of Geyve district of Sakarya and stayed there for about 4 years. Meanwhile, I completed my high school education. Later, we settled in the Mithatpaşa neighborhood of Adapazarı district. Now, I am studying Computer Engineering at Sakarya University Computer and Information Engineering Faculty.
